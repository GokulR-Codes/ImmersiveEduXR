Hello! I'm analyzing the Engineering model loaded in your viewer. This is a helical gear assembly. What would you like to learn?
You
How does the involute tooth profile work?
EduXR Tutor
Great question! An involute profile is generated by unwrapping a string from a base circle. This ensures that as two gears mesh, the contact point moves along a fixed line — the line of action — maintaining a constant velocity ratio regardless of small center distance errors. Notice the highlighted yellow region on the 3D model showing the contact zone.
You
